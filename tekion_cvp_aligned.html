Customer Value Review
The Customer Value Plan is a living account document, not a meeting. It defines what a dealer is trying to achieve, where they are in their Tekion journey, and what the CVM and CVA will do next to move them forward. This Phase 1 template activates sections 1 through 3. Sections 4 through 7 will be introduced as operational infrastructure and cross-functional alignment mature.

When to Update This Plan

The CVP is updated when something changes — not on a fixed calendar. Any of the following should trigger a review and update:

Dealer maturity tier shifts New module goes live Risk flag raised by CVM or CVA Renewal window opens CVR completed Sponsor or key contact changes Escalation resolved New dealer goal surfaces in conversation
